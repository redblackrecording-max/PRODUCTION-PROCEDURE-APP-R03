Buffer Rule

  • Start each new pulse with 30 machines worth of sub-assemblies.
  • Every day, replace what the main build consumed.
  • Shortages go to Antoine before they become build blockers.

SOP Rule

  • During dry-run week, every unclear step gets photographed or written down, and the Friday end-to-end run-through gets timed.
  • Emma and Emily control guide gaps.
  • Conveyor notes are especially important because the conveyor SOP is not complete yet.
